To The Love of My Life

Isaac, you will always be the cookies to my cream, the rice to my sushi, and the passionfruit to my iced tea.

I promised I’ll always be right by your side, always loving you and supporting you because you’re the only guy I wanna do life with. That is, and will always be true.

I thought I knew what my favorite 2 colors were, but that was until I saw your beautiful hazel eyes; the ones I could get lost in for hours.

I don’t wanna spend a second without you; I adore you endlessly and I can't wait to be with you for the rest of my life, beary bear.
